Program Latihan Kami

Bodybuilding

Fokus pada pembentukan tubuh ideal melalui latihan hipertrofi otot, terinspirasi dari split latihan Chris Bumstead.

Jadwal Latihan Mingguan

  • Senin (Push): Bench Press, Incline Dumbbell Press, Overhead Press, Tricep Dips
  • Selasa (Pull): Deadlift, Pull-Ups, Barbell Rows, Bicep Curls
  • Rabu: Istirahat
  • Kamis (Legs): Squats, Leg Press, Romanian Deadlift, Calf Raises
  • Jumat (Shoulders): Military Press, Lateral Raises, Rear Delt Flyes
  • Sabtu (Arms): Close-Grip Bench, Hammer Curls, Tricep Pushdowns
  • Minggu: Istirahat

Powerlifting

Maksimalkan kekuatan melalui tiga gerakan utama: squat, bench press, dan deadlift, terinspirasi dari Jamal Browner.

Jadwal Latihan Mingguan

  • Senin (Squat): Squat (Heavy), Front Squat, Leg Press
  • Selasa (Bench): Bench Press (Heavy), Close-Grip Bench, Dumbbell Press
  • Rabu: Istirahat
  • Kamis (Deadlift): Deadlift (Heavy), Romanian Deadlift, Barbell Rows
  • Jumat (Accessory): Overhead Press, Weighted Pull-Ups, Core Work
  • Sabtu: Istirahat
  • Minggu: Light Squat/Bench, Mobility Work

Calisthenics

Kembangkan kekuatan dan fleksibilitas dengan latihan berat badan, terinspirasi dari Chris Heria.

Jadwal Latihan Mingguan

  • Senin (Upper Body): Pull-Ups, Push-Ups, Dips, Pike Push-Ups
  • Selasa (Lower Body): Bodyweight Squats, Lunges, Single-Leg Glute Bridges
  • Rabu: Istirahat
  • Kamis (Core): Plank Variations, Hanging Leg Raises, Russian Twists
  • Jumat (Skill Work): Handstand Practice, Muscle-Up Progressions
  • Sabtu (Full Body): Burpees, Pull-Ups, Push-Ups, Squats
  • Minggu: Istirahat
